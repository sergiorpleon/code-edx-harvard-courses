Welcome to the World of Chess

Chess is a strategy game played between two people. It is known for its depth and complexity, and has been played for centuries.

Learn its history.

Game elements

Dashboard

The chess board is made up of 64 squares, 32 black and 32 white. It is delimited by letters and numbers as we will see below:
  • Each row receives a name, for the first it is 1, for the second it is 2... for the eighth it is 8.
  • Each column is named a letter starting from a and ending with h.
  • Each box has a different name (a1, b1, c1, etc.) formed by the intersection of the column and the row.

Parts

Each of the two players has sixteen pieces (to differentiate them, one player's pieces are colored light and are called the white ones and the other ones are dark in color, and are called the black ones):
  • A king
  • A lady, commonly called a queen
  • Two bishops
  • Two horses
  • Two towers
  • Eight pawns
